💬 My Story
Hello everyone!! I’m the “developer” of this app. First of all, I’m not a real developer… Just a 16 year old who knows how to use AI and wants to solve a massive problem for free.
❓ Why did I make this?
Yes, there are apps that do this exact same stuff — but they are all paid. Every single one I tried always gave me a quiz, then amazing customer feedback, then hit me with a paywall.

This puts these apps out of reach for millions, including me. That’s when I had the idea to make this.

Hope this helps you guys. 🙂
💸 Always Free
This app is completely free because I don’t have any costs associated with running it.

If you want features like active blocking, leaderboards, etc. — those costs will still have to be covered. The app will remain free, but when I’m able to open up a donation page, they will come. 🙂
— Kylan ❤
